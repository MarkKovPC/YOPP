About Us

Yale Petrol is an organization dedicated to the study and discussion of energy markets, petroleum policy, and the evolving landscape of global fuel resources.

We bring together students, researchers, and industry professionals to foster informed dialogue about the future of energy.

What We Do

Research

In-depth analysis of petroleum markets, energy policy, and emerging technologies.

Workshops and resources for students interested in the energy sector.
